# foundations
## ape together strong

a repo I put together so other people may accelerate their own learning journey by
taking a look at some of the resources that helped me.

a little bit about me: I have been a DevOps engineer for the past 5 years. I have a strong
sense of imposter syndrome and recently learned that imposter syndrome is just your body
pushing you to greatness. It is a cry to get stronger, to grow, & to learn.

if you don't feel the imposter syndrome, you either are too early in your journey to realize the vast knowledge you don't possess or simiply the best at
whatever it is you do, most likely the former.
